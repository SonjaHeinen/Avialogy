Avialogy (Europe) builds on the premise that there is a relationship between primary systems of aerial commotion and phenomena in the human world. This study interprets movements and relative positions of powered, fixed-wing machines in cruising altitude. Simply put: It's your horoscope based on aircraft, not star constellations.
